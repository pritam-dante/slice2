Glossary

Payment Obligations

Please ensure that at least the Minimum Amount Due (MAD) is paid by the Due Date. Failure to do so within 3 days of the due date will result in reporting to credit bureaus as a defaulter.

Please refer to your MITC (Most Important Terms and Conditions) available on slice app or website for details on how your MAD is calculated.

Finance Charges

If any portion from the total bill due remains unpaid after the payment due date, interest charges may apply following the average daily reducing balance method

Paying only the minimum amount each month may result in long-term debt with compounded interest and loss of interest-free credit period benefits

Credit Card Security

To immediately block your card due to loss, theft, or suspicious activity, contact our customer support through the slice app or call on 080 4832 9999.

Card and UPI spends

All money you spent using your slice credit card or UPI during this statement's billing cycle.

Refunds

Any transaction refunds or reversals returned to your credit card account.

Cashback

Any reward money credited to your credit card account.

Advance payments

Any advance repayments you made against your credit card balance during this statement's billing cycle are credited to your slice credit card account.

Ongoing EMIs

Monthly installment payments due for active sliced transactions or any active EMIs on your credit card.

Interest & charges

Fees applicable on your credit card account for late payments, bank transfers, surcharges, finance charges, etc.

Carry forward from July

Any outstanding balance from your previous month's statement that remains unpaid is carried forward.
